## About

FinTech Product Manager specialized in **Payments & APIs** — I operate at the intersection of product thinking and technical execution.

I build payment prototypes end-to-end: from architecture decisions to working demos. My focus is understanding the real trade-offs behind payment systems — routing logic, failure modes, retry strategies, B2B vs B2C marketplace constraints — not just the theory.

- Currently building **Switchpay**, a payment orchestration sandbox
- Learning **Ruby on Rails** to go deeper on backend
- Background: EDHEC Business School
